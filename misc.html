Business + Project Management + Tools + Etc.

** SPECIAL PRO GAME DEV TIP! ** Always record your work! Get screenshots and videos of your games so you can put them on your website/portfolio. This is because a) you are going to want to show off your work at some point if you are looking for a job and b) images and videos won't become outdated as quickly as the software the game uses. So when you find that you can no longer run the game on the computer or the code base is so outdated you might as well start the project from scratch, you are going to wish you took some screenshots and recorded some gameplay.

Gyazo - This is a great tool for capturing screenshots and gifs of your work so you can share them on Twitter or save for your portfolio.

Twitter - Be active on Twitter and other social media. You need to be marketing your games and your work.

Twitch - Stream development of your game/art/music/etc. This is a great way to market your game AND the cool thing is that you are still making it as you do it. People these days are really interested in a game's development processes and like to participate/get to know the developer.
